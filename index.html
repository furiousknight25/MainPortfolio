The purpose of my website is to display my growth as a writer through the course. I hope to showcase my ability to write different kinds of papers, use different styles of citing and types of research material, and express my ideas clearly.

  • My biggest weakest in writing is sumarizing research into consise, well sourced paragraphs. Often I do lots of research and come up with a 1000 things to say, but in the end it turns my paper into ramblings.
  • 2 writers who have inspried me are Nicky Case and Mrs. Kartak. What I love about both of these writers is the emphasis on teaching through multimodal ways. Both teachers put lots of effort into conveying a message in an entertaining and quick way. I dream to create papers and work that can teach at the same efficeny.
